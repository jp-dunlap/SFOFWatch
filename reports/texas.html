Key Affiliated Official: Glenn Hegar (Comptroller of Public Accounts)

Status: Core Member State

Operational Summary:

Texas is a key battleground for SFOF's anti-ESG campaign. Comptroller Glenn Hegar is a prominent and active member, leveraging his position to implement policies that align directly with SFOF's agenda. The state has been a pioneer in promoting and passing ALEC-drafted model legislation targeting financial firms that SFOF considers hostile to the fossil fuel industry.

Key Actions:

  • Enactment of SB 13 (2021): A landmark piece of legislation, reflecting ALEC's "Eliminate Political Boycotts Act," which requires state entities like pension funds to divest from companies that "boycott" fossil fuel producers.
  • Divestment Threats: Comptroller Hegar has publicly listed financial firms, including BlackRock, for potential divestment, creating significant pressure on the financial industry.
  • Public Advocacy: Serves as a key signatory on numerous SFOF-organized national sign-on letters targeting federal regulators and corporate entities.
