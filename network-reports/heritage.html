The Heritage Foundation

A leading conservative think tank providing the ideological and legal foundation for the anti-ESG campaign. It acts as both a funder and institutional patron for SFOF.

Key Activities

  • Model Legislation: Developed the "State Pension Fiduciary Duty Act."
  • The "Sole Interest" Doctrine: This model bill codifies a narrow interpretation of fiduciary duty, mandating that investment managers consider "only financial factors" and explicitly excluding social or ideological interests.
  • Institutional Support: Awarded SFOF a $100,000 "Innovation Prize" in 2022.
