DonorsTrust

Described as the "dark money ATM of the political right." As a Donor-Advised Fund (DAF), it allows donors to anonymously funnel money to conservative causes.

Role in the Network

DonorsTrust is a central financial clearinghouse for the anti-ESG movement, funding the entire ecosystem.

  • To SFOF: Granted $405,000 (2022-2023).
  • To Consumers' Research: Granted $6.5 million (2023).
  • To State Policy Network (SPN): Granted $10.5 million (2023).

It receives significant funding from other hubs, such as Leonard Leo's Marble Freedom Trust, adding layers of obfuscation between the original donors and the final recipients.
