State Financial Officers Foundation (SFOF)

SFOF is the operational command center for the national anti-ESG campaign. Founded in 2012, it underwent a strategic transformation after 2020, pivoting sharply to combat "woke capitalism."

Key Functions

  • Coordination Hub: Connects opaque funding streams from ideological foundations and corporate donors to specific political actions by state treasurers.
  • Weaponizing State Funds: Leverages the financial power of state treasuries and pension funds to exert economic pressure on corporations.
  • Lobbying Arm: Created SFOF Action (501c4) in 2024 to engage in unlimited lobbying and direct political activity.

Financial Growth

SFOF's revenue exploded after its anti-ESG pivot, reaching $2.85 million in 2023. 59% of its funding comes from foundations, and 31% from corporate sponsorships.

Controversies

Accused in an August 2024 SEC complaint of engaging in an illegal "pay-to-play" scheme, where corporate sponsors allegedly receive favorable treatment from SFOF members.
