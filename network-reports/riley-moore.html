Riley Moore (Former West Virginia Treasurer)

Influential SFOF member and the "first mover" in the anti-ESG movement, developing the tactical blueprint replicated nationally.

Key Actions

  • Jan 2022: Became the first elected official in America to divest state funds from BlackRock over ESG policies.
  • July 2022: Created West Virginia's first "Restricted Financial Institutions List".
  • The Blacklist: Formally blacklisted five major institutions (including BlackRock, JPMorgan Chase, Wells Fargo) from state banking contracts.

Impact

Moore actively coordinated with other SFOF members to scale his strategy. The "Restricted List" model provided a tangible, replicable mechanism for other states to use.
