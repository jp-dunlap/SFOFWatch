Glenn Hegar (Texas Comptroller)

SFOF Member and former ALEC member. Leads the implementation of Texas's sweeping anti-ESG legislation (SB 13).

Key Actions

  • Implementation of SB 13 (2021): Tasked with creating and maintaining a list of financial companies that "boycott energy companies."

Financial Fallout

The implementation of SB 13 led to the exit of major municipal bond underwriters. Studies found this resulted in $303M-$532M in increased borrowing costs for Texas municipalities and $669M in lost economic activity (FY22-23).

"Pay-to-Play" Allegations

The CfA SEC complaint suggests that SFOF "Diamond" sponsor Public Trust Advisors (PTA) benefited from the new regulatory environment created by SB 13.
