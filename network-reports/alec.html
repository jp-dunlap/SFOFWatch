American Legislative Exchange Council (ALEC)

A corporate bill mill deeply integrated with SFOF, serving as the primary legislative vehicle for the anti-ESG campaign.

Integration with SFOF

  • ALEC CEO Lisa Nelson sits on SFOF's board of directors.
  • They share the same primary funders (Bradley, DonorsTrust, Koch).

Role in the Network: The Legislative Mill

ALEC provides the legislative templates, notably the "Eliminate Political Boycotts Act." This model bill reframes corporate risk management as a politically motivated "economic boycott," allowing state officials to punish firms by prohibiting state contracts.
